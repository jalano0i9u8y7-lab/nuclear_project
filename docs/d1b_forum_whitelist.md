- SeekingAlpha Comments (評論區)

## Chinese Forums (TW)

## Japanese Forums (JP)
- 5ch (原 2ch) 市況板
- Yahoo! ファイナンス掲示板
- みんなの株式 (minkabu.jp)

## SSOT Constraints
- **Whitelist-only**: Only sources listed above are permitted.
- **Daily Cap**: Each forum is capped at 50 hot discussions per day.
- **Filters**:
  - Engagement: Recency < 24 hours.
  - Discussion: Minimum 10 replies.
- **Total Volume**: Maximum 300 total forum items per day across all sources.
- **Strict Separation**: D1-A (News) and D1-B (Forums) must remain entirely separate pipelines to prevent pollution of authoritative news with retail sentiment.
